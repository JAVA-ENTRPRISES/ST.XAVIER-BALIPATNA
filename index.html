About Us

Located in the peaceful surroundings of Balipatna in Odisha, **St. Xavier High School** stands as a growing center of quality education and holistic development. Established in **2018**, the school was founded with a vision to provide modern, value-based learning that nurtures knowledge, character, and leadership among students. St. Xavier, Balipatna focuses on academic excellence while encouraging participation in sports, cultural activities, and skill-based learning to ensure the all-round development of every child. With dedicated teachers, a disciplined environment, and student-friendly facilities, the school strives to create confident, responsible, and future-ready individuals, making it one of the promising educational institutions in the Balipatna region. 🌱📚
